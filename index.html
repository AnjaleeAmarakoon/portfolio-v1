Tech Bar

Automated Bartender

Tech Bar - Automated Bartender

TechBar is an automated bartender machine developed using Arduino Mega, designed to revolutionize the bartending experience with precise motor control, a rotatable custom tray, and a user-friendly interface featuring an LCD, LEDs, and buttons. Key features include NEMA17 stepper motors for accurate movement, IR sensors to detect glass placement, a limit switch for tray positioning, auditory buzzer feedback, and an optional mixing arm. This innovative project enhances drink customization, operational efficiency, and customer interaction, developed as a first-year hardware project at the University of Moratuwa under expert guidance.
